About Us

The Bhutanese Association in Switzerland (BAS) is a community-driven, non-political, and non-profit organization that brings together Bhutanese individuals and families living in Switzerland. Its main mission is to preserve Bhutanese culture, foster a sense of community, and support Bhutanese people—especially youth and newcomers—in their integration while maintaining ties to their heritage.

Objectives of BAS

  • Promote and preserve Bhutanese culture and values
  • Celebrate national and cultural events (e.g. Losar, National Day)
  • Support Bhutanese students and families in Switzerland
  • Create platforms for inter-generational bonding
  • Collaborate with the Embassy and Swiss organizations to represent Bhutan
